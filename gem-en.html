G.E.M.

G.E.M. Deng Ziqi is a Chinese pop singer-songwriter and music producer. Her music often brings together a highly recognizable voice, piano textures, pop melodies, and emotional force.

Profile

Profile

Deng Ziqi, born Gloria Tang Sze-wing, performs under the name G.E.M., short for "Get Everybody Moving." Born in Shanghai and raised in Hong Kong, she debuted as a singer in 2008 and later reached a much wider Chinese-speaking audience through original songs, live vocals, and strong stage presence.

Her music crosses pop, R&B, rock, and electronic elements, and she is deeply involved in songwriting. From early emotional ballads to later concept albums and large-scale tours, her work carries a clear personal narrative.
